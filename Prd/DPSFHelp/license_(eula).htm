The official legalese version of the DPSF End User License Agreement (EULA) can be found further down, but directly below is a brief description of the EULA in simple English.  In the event of any discrepancies between the simple and official EULA, the official EULA will take precedence.

 

Simple EULA Description

 

Conditions

 

DPSF is free to use in your applications, whether they are free or commercial for profit.  All released games/products must mention DPSF somewhere in the product.  This may be through the provided DPSF splash screen particle system (players may skip the splash screen by pressing a button), displaying the DPSF logo (image), or simply mentioning DPSF in text.  Both the DPSF logo and splash screen particle system can be found in the DPSF Logos directory (typically C:\DPSF\Logos).  If you are only mentioning DPSF in your product's credits, then the credits should be accessible from the main menu.

 

 

Redistribution

 

You may redistribute DPSF to others for them to use in their projects, as long as you do so by redistributing the DPSF installer itself.  This ensures that the end-users receive all DPSF related files (e.g. dll files, help documentation, tutorials and demos, etc.) and that they agree to the DPSF EULA included in the installer before using DPSF.
